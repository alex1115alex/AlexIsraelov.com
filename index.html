About Myself

I’ve spent the last few years hacking AI-powered AR tech to overclock the human brain. From smart glasses that project knowledge straight to your eyeballs to wearables that augment everyday life, my work is all about pushing the boundaries of human-computer interaction. Related projects:

Convoscope - Smart glasses intelligence tools. Proactively answers questions and feeds you useful information before you can even ask. Demo video here.

SmartGlassesManager - A universal smart glasses interface, providing a centralized way for app developers to write one app that runs on any pair of smart glasses.

OpenSourceSmartGlasses - Smart glasses with display, microphone, wireless Bluetooth, prescription lenses, and hours of battery life. Hosts a suite of apps including live language translation, contextual search engine, intelligent assistant, and web search.

Telescope - My first smart glasses - includes a display, wireless bluetooth, and a companion app. It displays the time, your phone's notifications, and a teleprompter.

SmartGlassesDriveAssistant - Smart glasses app to display your vehicle's speed and engine RPM. It works in conjunction with the SmartGlassesManager. Demo video here.
